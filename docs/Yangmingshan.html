Hot Spring

Because this mountain is a member of Tatun volcano group, it has lava under to heat the water up. The hot spring of here is very famous in Taiwan, it is a must-do if you visit here.

Flowers

When spring comes, Taiwanese people like to go watch flowers here. Each month has different flowers to watch, if you are interested in watching flowers, you can click "more information" to get to know more about it.
